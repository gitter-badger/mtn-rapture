Mountain Rapture

This is a game written in Lua using a wonderful 2D game creation framework known as LOVE, inspired by classic JRPGs with a story built out of a cross-cultural worldwide motif known in English as "king in the mountain", found in many folk tales, legends, and mythologies. The story is based upon the return of a hero to their people in a time of dire need, making a grand effort to save them from their ill fate.
